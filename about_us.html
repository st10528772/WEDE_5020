About Us

Organisation's History

Makro was established in 1971 in Germiston as South Africa's first warehouse store that sold goods to the public, operating under Dutch Makro. In 2011, Walmart, an American retail corporation, bought the majority of Makro's shares, making it part of the global Walmart group. Massmart is Makro's parent company in South Africa and was established in 1990. In 2024, Massmart announced plans to convert several Game stores into shopping centres, with the first conversion taking place at East Point Shopping Centre in Boksburg.

Mission Statement

Makro is a store that customers always return to, with the confidence that they will receive quality products and outstanding service — for both individuals and businesses.

Vision Statement

Makro aims to continuously adapt to changes in the market and introduce new ideas, products, and services — with the goal of becoming the leading retailer in its market sector.
