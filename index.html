Our Story

Makro is one of South Africa's leading warehouse retailers, part of the Massmart group. We supply quality products at wholesale prices to both individuals and businesses across the country. Since 1971, Makro has been helping South Africans save more by buying smarter. Today, as part of Massmart and Walmart, we combine bulk buying power with modern retail convenience.
